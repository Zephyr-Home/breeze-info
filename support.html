breeZe App – Support

Last updated: July 2025

Need Help?

If you experience any issues or have questions about using the BreeZe app, please try the following steps:

  • Restart the app to refresh your session.
  • Ensure your device is connected to the internet.
  • Check for any updates to the app in your device’s app management system.
  • Verify your login credentials if you have trouble signing in.

Contact Support

If the issue persists, reach out to our support team for assistance:
