BreeZe App – Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

The BreeZe app is designed for internal use by employees and contractors of Zephyr. It is distributed privately through Apple's Custom App program and is not available to the general public.

What Data We Collect

  • Email address (used to authenticate with Zephyr’s Google Workspace)
  • Technician and job-related data (retrieved via ServiceTitan APIs)
  • App usage logs (used for diagnostics and support)

How We Use Your Data

All collected data is used solely to support job assignments and technician workflows. No personal data is shared with third parties.

Data Security

All authentication and data transfers are encrypted. Access is restricted to authorized users within Zephyr’s infrastructure.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact Zephyr IT at it-support@zephyrhome.com.
